About us!

What is this Website for?

This website shows you what pubs around Ireland are disabled accessible!

Why make it?

We wanted people to be able to look up pubs in a certain area and be able to tell if the pub you want to visit is wheelchair accessible!

Who are you guys?

We are students from NCI who want to make it easier for people that use wheelchair to find pubs that has wheelchair accessibility
